# SaaSCoop

**Technology as a Common Good.**

SaaSCoop is an open-source cooperative-oriented initiative dedicated to building software, artificial intelligence and digital education in an open, sustainable and community-driven way.

## Mission

To develop digital and artificial intelligence solutions in a cooperative, transparent and sustainable way, promoting technological sovereignty, human-centered work and democratic access to technology as a common good.

## What we are building

- Open source solutions
- Sustainable artificial intelligence
- Open digital education
- Tools for communities
- Transparent governance
- Community funding models

## Main projects

- FreireAI
- EmpathyAI
- SaaSCoop Education
- GitHub Governance
- SaaSCoop Standards

## Status

SaaSCoop is currently in a structuring phase and is not yet presented here as a formally incorporated cooperative.
