# Governance

SaaSCoop adopts a transparent, participatory and community-oriented governance approach.

## Roles

- Supporter
- Community member
- Contributor
- Maintainer
- Future cooperative member

## Decisions

Decisions should preferably be documented through:

- Issues
- Discussions
- Pull Requests
- ADRs
- Public roadmaps

## Cautions

SaaSCoop avoids conflicts of interest, does not operate with the public sector and does not present itself as a formally incorporated cooperative before proper legal validation.
