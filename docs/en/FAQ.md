# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is SaaSCoop already a cooperative?

Not yet. SaaSCoop is currently structuring its legal, financial and governance model.

## Does SaaSCoop operate with the public sector?

No. SaaSCoop does not operate with the public sector.

## Why open source?

Because we believe technology should be shared as open knowledge and a common good.

## How can I contribute?

You can contribute with code, documentation, translation, research, testing, design, education or community participation.

## What is Open Collective?

It is a platform used by open source communities to receive and manage funds transparently.
