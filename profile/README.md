# SaaSCoop

**SaaSCoop** is an open source cooperative initiative focused on building digital and AI solutions as common goods.

We are structuring a community-driven model that combines:

- open source software
- cooperative governance
- digital education
- sustainable artificial intelligence
- community funding
- dignified work
- long-term technological sovereignty

## Mission

To develop digital and artificial intelligence solutions in a cooperative, transparent and sustainable way, promoting technological sovereignty, human-centered work and democratic access to technology as a common good.

## What we build

SaaSCoop develops and supports open source initiatives related to:

- educational AI
- ethical and sustainable AI
- accessibility and inclusion
- data, business intelligence and automation
- open digital tools for communities
- technical education and digital literacy

## Main initiatives

### FreireAI

An educational AI initiative inspired by Paulo Freire’s legacy, focused on emancipatory education, literacy and access to knowledge.

### EmpathyAI

An AI initiative focused on supporting neurodivergent people, especially autistic adults, in communication, decision-making and workplace inclusion.

### SaaSCoop Education

An open education channel designed to share knowledge about GitHub, artificial intelligence, software engineering, data and digital cooperation.

### SaaSCoop Governance

A transparent governance initiative to document decisions, roles, responsibilities and community participation.

- supporting the project financially

## Governance approach

SaaSCoop separates participation roles from the beginning:

- **Supporter**: contributes financially or promotes the initiative
- **Community member**: participates in discussions and prioritization
- **Contributor**: contributes with code, documentation, design, research or testing
- **Maintainer**: helps review, organize and maintain repositories
- **Future cooperative member**: participates in the formal cooperative work and governance structure, once legally established

SaaSCoop is currently an initiative in structuring phase and is not yet presented here as a formally incorporated cooperative.
